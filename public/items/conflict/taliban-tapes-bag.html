Al-Qaeda Training Tape Sack

This is the container used to transport al-Qaeda training tapes from remote areas of Afghanistan. Tapes were stored in an al-Qaeda safe house and acquired by CNN. They showed scenes of Osama bin Laden interacting with his aides and the training of his soldiers. Some of the tapes showchemical weapons testing on dogs.
