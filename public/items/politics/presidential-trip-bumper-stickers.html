Presidential Trip Bumper Stickers

These bumper stickers were used by CNN crews to identify technical gear transported on Presidential Trips during the early 1980’s. The two examples in the collection were obtained during President Reagan’s trip to China in 1984.

CNN Cameraman, Gary Shore and I were part of the CNN team that traveled to China with President Reagan in 1984. Along with nearly 600 other journalists, Gary and I captured six historic days of events surrounding the visit.
