1988 Press Placard

This press placard was issued to CNN camera crews assigned to the CNN Presidential Debate in Los Angeles on October 13, 1988. Bernard Shaw moderated the debate and made headlines when he asked Governor Dukakis, "If Kitty Dukakis were raped and murdered, would you favor an irrevocable death penalty for the killer?"
