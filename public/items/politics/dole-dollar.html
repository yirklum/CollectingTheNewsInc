Bob Dole Novelty Million Dollar Bill

Believe it or not, there is an entire industry in America devoted to the making of novelty one million-dollar bills. They have bills in holiday themes, sports themes and historical themes. You want a one-million-dollar bill with a Chihuahua on it? They got it! You want a million-dollar bill celebrating Apollo 11? It can be ordered. The political bills are among the most popular. This Bob Dole One Million Dollar Bill was obtained from a vender outside one of the Bob Dole 1996 presidential campaign events. If you look hard enough, you can find a Zillion Dollar Federal Bailout Dollar Bill!

$100,000 is the largest legal denomination of U.S. currency ever printed. It was a gold certificate and made between 1934 and 1935.
