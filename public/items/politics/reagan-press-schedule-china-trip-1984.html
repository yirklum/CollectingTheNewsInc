1984 Reagan China Trip Press Schedule

This is the official Presidential Schedule for Ronald Reagan’s trip to the People’s Republic of China, which lasted from April 26 to May 1, 1984. Known by press crews as “the bible”, the booklet contains the proposed schedule for the President’s trip. It was issued by the Press Office with the warning that the information was for planning purposes only, not for public distribution.
