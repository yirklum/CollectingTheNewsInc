1996 Republican Convention Meal Pass

With the number of editorial and technical staff involved in producing something as massive as a national political convention, the logistical challenges providing meals gets complicated. The CNN Special Events team attempt to provide order by issuing meal passes for access to daily catering for workers unable to leave the convention center.
