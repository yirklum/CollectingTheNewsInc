1976 Cattle for Carter Signed Poster

This poster was from Jimmy Carter's presidential campaign of 1976. Carter was Governor of Georgia at the time and the poster featured the Governor and his daughter, Amy. Years after the election I was able to get the poster signed by President Carter.
