Dole Press Corps Blue Fog Society Hat

The Blue Fog Society was a group of journalists within the Dole/Kemp traveling press corps that had an affinity for good cigars. During breaks in the coverage, usually at the RON (Remainder of Night Hotel), the group would find an isolated spot at the hotel and share a few good cigars and stories from the road. It designed a hat to commemorate the group.
