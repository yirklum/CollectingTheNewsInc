Dole-Kemp Shirt Signed by Frank Sesno

This is a Dole/Kemp campaign shirt signed by Frank Sesno on August 9, 1996! Frank Sesno is a former CNN correspondent, anchor and Washington Bureau Chief. The shirt had just been delivered and Dole announced Kemp as his running mate on August 10, 1996.

On Aug. 10, 1996, U.S. Senator Bob Dole announces former Secretary of Housing and Urban Development Jack Kemp as his vice-presidential running mate for the 1996 presidential campaign.

Frank Sesno
