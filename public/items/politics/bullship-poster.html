Dole Press Corps "Bullship" Banner

The 1996 Dole/Kemp Campaign aircraft was called Leadership One. It was in an era when the press corps still travelled with the candidate on the same plane. All the writers, television crews, still photographers and radio reporters were pretty much based in the rear of the aircraft. To memorialize the daily grind of covering a presidential campaign, I designed a banner for the press section of the campaign aircraft and invited all journalists who came onboard to sign it. It was also signed by the candidate and flight crew.
