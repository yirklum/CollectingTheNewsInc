Whitewater Promotional Brochure

This promotional brochure for land along the White River near Flippin, Arkansas led to an investigation that resulted in an impeachment of a president of the United States. Incorporated by Jim and Susan McDougal as the Whitewater Development Corporation in 1979, the plan was to sell vacation property and share the investment with business partners Bill and Hillary Clinton. Before the lots could be sold, interest rates rose to 20% and the business eventually turned into a bad investment.
